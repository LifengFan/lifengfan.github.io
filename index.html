About Me

I'm a PhD student advised by Prof. Song-Chun Zhu in Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy (VCLA) in the Department of Statistics at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). My research interests focus on computer vision and cognition, particularly in the areas of 3D scene understanding and social cue understanding. My goal is to help machines reach human-level understanding from visual input.

Previously, I received a M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA and a B.E. in Communication Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology.
